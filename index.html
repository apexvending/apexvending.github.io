Talk to us directly
Is there any cost or burden on our organization?+
None. The machine, installation, maintenance, and restocking are all provided at zero cost. There are no fees, no contracts, and nothing for you to manage. We earn through product sales—so your team's satisfaction is our business model.
How do you decide which products to stock?+
We start by learning your team's preferences, dietary needs, and favorite brands. Once the machine is live, real-time sales data takes over—we use it continuously to refine your selection, so the machine gets smarter over time.
How often is the machine restocked?+
Restocking is driven by data, not a fixed calendar. Our machines alert us when inventory reaches a threshold, so we restock proactively—before anything runs out. Most locations are serviced multiple times per week.
What do products cost?+
Pricing is competitive with a convenience store—accessible to everyone on your team. Beverages typically range from $1.50–$3.50 and snacks from $1.25–$3.00. We work with each partner to make sure pricing feels fair for their specific environment.
What payment methods are accepted?+
All modern contactless methods: Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay, and tap-to-pay credit and debit cards. Cash is accepted on most machines. The experience is designed to be frictionless for everyone.
What if something goes wrong?+
Our machines run remote diagnostics continuously—we typically detect and respond to issues before you notice them. Every request or concern is acknowledged within 24 hours, guaranteed.
Can we change what's stocked over time?+
Absolutely. Want something new, healthier options, or a seasonal change? Just let us know. We make changes quickly—no bureaucracy, no waiting.
